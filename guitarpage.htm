A few nights ago, I was talking with my good friend James about playing guitar. Just general converstation about things that each of us has gone through during the learning process; steps and paths we've taken to learn the instrument, resources we've used, and songs we play just because they're easy and fun to play and no one can tell us to stop! Because in the end, isn't that what it's all about?

I haven't played guitar for very long, about 2 years. I'm not very good by any means; I can find my way around the fretboard, play the 8 basic chords, and play power chords till no end. At least that's where I feel I'm at, for now. But during this conversation I had realized that I've come across and used some amazing resources over the last couple years. Lessons, techniques, chord charts, tutorials and more information than I could ever imagine. I was going to pass on some of these resources to my buddy, but then I thought, why not post it on the website? So I'm going to do my best to list some of the best resources that helped me learn to play guitar.
