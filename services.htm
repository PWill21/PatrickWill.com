We live in a vast technological world, and every day brings forth new aspects of an increasingly digital life. Our lives are powered by 1's and 0's in more ways than we could have ever thought possible, even just a few short years ago. Digital technology moves staggeringly fast, and it's impossible to keep up with every amazing new advancement. In fact, most people find themselves months, if not years, behind the latest trends. As a result, a large percentage of people are introduced to these trends on an as-needed, or even forced, basis. Forced or not, we rely on these aspects more and more every day. They exist to make our lives easier, more efficient, and simpler. The ultimate goal is to benefit us as a society. Of course, not everything can be perfect, and unfortunately our tech lives are often interrupted by some form of failure. The good thing is, there is almost always a solution to these failures. My goal is to provide these solutions, both before and after they happen, and also to prevent them.
